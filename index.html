    About Us

    SANDESH- "Social-Awareness , National-Development and Educational -Standard - High-Tech" is Registered under Societies Registration Act, 1860 with Registration Number: "No.MC.Soc.Reg.SDO(C) Padhar -2010/-03 dtd 19.01.2010." The objectives of the society are generally to promote all aspect of education stander high-Tech throughout the country and to promote all aspect of education stander high tech throughout country and contributing in social awareness, National Development.

    Our Mission

    The objectives of the society are generally to promote all aspect of education stander high-Tech throughout the country in developing, in the accordance with the social awareness, integrated approaches to solve the problem of National Development and provide the incentive and equipments to attract youth to educational development services and undertake researches in science or applied sciences related to the work of society.

    Our Plan

    Our main plan is to educate rural and backward area children, provide general education, computer education. Women Empowerment, Social awareness, Road safety, personal Hygiene, Sexual Harassment, upliftment of poor people, Skill Development,National Development projects, Organize the camps for left out illiterate people to educate them in off time, Modernize the education system.

    Our Vision

    To make use of the services of the graduates and students on rational bases in the agriculture , educational and other services and activities of the societies.To bringing change in the lives of underprivileged children, youth and women, with a life-cycle approach of development. Empower underprivileged children, youth and women through relevant education, healthcare and market-focused livelihood programmes

    Social Work

    Education

    To establish school for children general education, computer education in rural & backward area. Provide basic and computer based education for children

    Women Empowerment

    Provide free basic and advance education, Computer Education,free skill based training for poor girl. Financial aid for marriage also.

    Health

    Organise Health care camps and drives time to time for better health and provide medicines and medical consultancy to poor and rural people

    UnPrivileged People

    Provide education, medical facilities, computer education to the unprivileged and backward disseminated people like BPL family's women and children

    Sexual Harassment

    Took strict action against Sexual harassment and domestic violence, coordinating with local bodies and administration for the same.

    National Development

    Contribute in National Development programs, spread word about new schemes Contributing in Road Safety,social awareness, Clean India etc.

    Digital India

    We promote Digital India and make in India products. Also contribute in digital India Campaign , provide computer based education and skills for rural and poor people

    Anti-Drug Campaign

    Organise awareness campaigns about anti-drug activities and spread awareness about harm of drug abuse and addiction, take necessary actions against such issues.

    Empower Unassisted

    Empowering Unassisted is one of the main motive, by providing them basic facilities education and work as per skills so they can live their life in a better way.
